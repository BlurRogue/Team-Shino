    10/15/2023 - Game Update

    Game Project Announced


    Cyber Swarm Announced

    We're proud to announce the production of our game Cyber Swarm. It's a tower defense game based on a futuristic Earth, battling against a robot alien invasion.

    Roadmap

    For the next few months our team will work on the production and release of this game. This is the current roadmap we have planned

    • Month 1: Pre-production and Planning -

    • Week 1-2: Conceptualization and Idea Refinement

    • Week 3-4: Team Formation and Prototyping

    • Month 2: Production -

    • Week 1-2: Asset Production

    • Week 3-4: Core Gameplay Development

    • Week 5-6: Level Design

    • Week 7-8: UI/UX Development

    • Month 3: Testing and Polishing -

    • Week 1-2: Alpha Testing

    • Week 3-4: Beta Testing and Iteration

    • Week 5-6: Optimization and Performance

    • Week 7-8: Final Polish and QA

    • Launch and Post-launch Activities -

    • Release the game on targeted platforms.
