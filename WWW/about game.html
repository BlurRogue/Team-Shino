    General

    Cyber Swarm is a tower defense strategy game where the player's objective is to defend the end of the path from waves of incoming enemies. You will have to strategically place defensive turrets along the path that enemies follow. These towers have various abilities such as shooting projectiles, slowing down enemies, or causing area-of-effect damage. You will have have limited resources, specifically scrap, to build and upgrade towers,requiring strategic decision-making to optimize defenses against different types of enemies. As the game progresses, enemies become more numerous and powerful, challenging players to adapt and upgrade their defenses accordingly. The goal is to prevent the enemies from reaching a designated endpoint. Good luck and have fun!

    Story

    Welcome to the adrenaline-pumping realm of Cyber Swarm! Defend against relentless waves by strategically placing towers. Construct your defense, balancing upgrades and resource management. Learn each tower's strengths and weaknesses, adapting your strategy to counter diverse enemies. From fast-moving foes to armored behemoths, victory demands strategic prowess. Utilize resources wisely and tailor your defenses to conquer each wave. Only the most astute commanders will emerge triumphant in this tower defense epic. Good luck!

    The basic turret serves as the foundation for strategic defense. Utilizing scrap, players can upgrade it into specialized variations such as the explosive missile turret, the fiery flame turret, the strategic command center and many more. Each upgrade transforms the battlefield dynamics, requiring players to choose wisely in the face of relentless enemy waves. The game hinges on your tactical decisions and the evolution of these powerful turrets. Will you withstand the onslaught and emerge victorious? The challenge awaits!
